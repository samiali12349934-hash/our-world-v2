World War II (1939–1945)

Beginning

WW2 began when Germany invaded Poland in 1939 under Adolf Hitler.

Axis vs Allies

Axis Powers: Germany, Italy, Japan. Allies: USA, UK, Soviet Union, France.

Major Events

The Holocaust, Pearl Harbor attack, D-Day invasion, and atomic bombs on Hiroshima and Nagasaki.

End of War

The war ended in 1945 after Germany and Japan surrendered.

Aftermath

The United Nations was created. Europe was divided, leading to the Cold War.
