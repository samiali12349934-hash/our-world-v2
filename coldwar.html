Cold War (1947–1991)

Background

The Cold War was a political and military tension between the United States and the Soviet Union.

Nuclear Arms Race

Both sides built nuclear weapons, creating fear of global destruction.

Proxy Wars

Korean War and Vietnam War were conflicts influenced by Cold War rivalry.

Berlin Wall

The Berlin Wall symbolized the division between East and West.

End of the Cold War

The Soviet Union collapsed in 1991, ending the Cold War.
