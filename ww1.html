⚔ World War I (1914–1918)

Causes of the War

The main causes were Militarism, Alliances, Imperialism, and Nationalism. European countries were competing for power and colonies.

The Spark

The assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand of Austria in 1914 triggered the war.

Main Sides

Allied Powers: Britain, France, Russia, USA

Central Powers: Germany, Austria-Hungary, Ottoman Empire

How It Ended

The war ended in 1918 with the Treaty of Versailles, which punished Germany and later contributed to World War II.
