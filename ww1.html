⚔️ World War I (1914–1918)

Main Causes

  • Militarism
  • Alliances
  • Imperialism
  • Nationalism

Trigger Event

The assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand in 1914 started the war.

Main Sides

Allied Powers vs Central Powers.

End of War

The Treaty of Versailles officially ended the war in 1919.

Impact

Millions died, empires collapsed, and it set the stage for World War II.
