Nature & Ecosystems

What is Nature?

Nature includes forests, oceans, rivers, animals, and ecosystems that support life.

Ecosystems

Major ecosystems include forests, deserts, oceans, wetlands, and grasslands.

Biodiversity

Biodiversity refers to the variety of living organisms on Earth. It is essential for balance.

Threats

Deforestation, pollution, climate change, and overexploitation threaten natural systems.

Conservation

Protected areas, wildlife conservation, and sustainable practices help protect nature.
