Nature & Ecosystems

What is Nature?

Nature includes forests, oceans, rivers, animals, and ecosystems supporting life.

Ecosystems

Major ecosystems: forests, deserts, oceans, wetlands, grasslands.

Biodiversity

Biodiversity is the variety of living organisms; essential for balance.

Threats

Deforestation, pollution, climate change, overexploitation threaten natural systems.

Conservation

Protected areas, wildlife conservation, sustainable practices help protect nature.
