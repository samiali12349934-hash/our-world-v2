What is Nature?

Nature includes all living and non-living things not made by humans. It includes forests, oceans, mountains, animals, and plants.

Types of Ecosystems

  • Forest Ecosystems
  • Marine Ecosystems
  • Desert Ecosystems
  • Grassland Ecosystems
  • Freshwater Ecosystems

Biodiversity

Biodiversity means the variety of life on Earth. It keeps ecosystems balanced.

Why Nature is Important

Nature provides oxygen, food, medicine, water, and climate regulation.

Conservation

Protecting forests, wildlife, and oceans ensures future generations survive.
