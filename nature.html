Nature

Nature includes forests, rivers, oceans, and wildlife. It provides oxygen and food for living beings. Healthy ecosystems support all animals and plants. Forests help regulate the climate. Oceans stabilize the planet’s temperature. Biodiversity ensures natural balance. Protecting nature protects future generations. Environmental care is essential for life. Every ecosystem is connected and valuable. Our actions influence nature’s survival.
