🌎 Major Countries of the World

🇪🇹 Ethiopia

Capital: Addis Ababa

Population: 120+ million

Ethiopia is one of the oldest civilizations in the world. It was never fully colonized and defeated Italy at the Battle of Adwa in 1896.

🇺🇸 United States

Capital: Washington, D.C.

Population: 330+ million

The United States is one of the largest economies in the world and played major roles in World War I, World War II, and the Cold War.

🇩🇪 Germany

Capital: Berlin

Population: 80+ million

Germany was central in both World Wars and is now one of Europe’s strongest economies.

🇨🇳 China

Capital: Beijing

Population: 1.4+ billion

China has the largest population in the world and one of the fastest-growing economies in modern history.

🇧🇷 Brazil

Capital: Brasília

Population: 210+ million

Brazil is the largest country in South America and home to the Amazon rainforest.

🇫🇷 France

Capital: Paris

France played major roles in both World Wars and is known for its culture, art, and history.

🇯🇵 Japan

Capital: Tokyo

Japan became a major world power in the 20th century and was involved in World War II.

🇷🇺 Russia

Capital: Moscow

Russia was part of the Soviet Union during the Cold War and remains one of the largest countries in the world.
