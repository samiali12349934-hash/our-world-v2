What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into air, water, and land, damaging ecosystems and human health.

Types

  • Air Pollution – smoke and gases
  • Water Pollution – chemicals in rivers & oceans
  • Soil Pollution – toxic waste on land
  • Noise Pollution – excessive sound
  • Light Pollution – too much artificial light

Effects

  • Respiratory & heart diseases
  • Waterborne illnesses
  • Habitat destruction
  • Biodiversity loss

Solutions

Use renewable energy, reduce plastics, recycle, and protect ecosystems.
