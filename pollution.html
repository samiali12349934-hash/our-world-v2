Pollution

Air Pollution

Factories, vehicles, and burning fuels release harmful gases that affect health.

Water Pollution

Industrial waste & plastic contaminate rivers & oceans, harming marine life.

Soil Pollution

Pesticides & chemicals damage soil fertility and food production.

Health Effects

Pollution causes respiratory diseases, heart problems, and environmental damage.

Solutions

Recycling, clean energy, environmental laws, and education reduce pollution.
