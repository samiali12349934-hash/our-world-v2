Pollution

Air Pollution

Factories, vehicles, and burning fuels release harmful gases that affect human health.

Water Pollution

Industrial waste and plastic contaminate rivers and oceans, harming marine life.

Soil Pollution

Pesticides and chemicals damage soil fertility and food production.

Health Effects

Pollution causes respiratory diseases, heart problems, and environmental damage.

Solutions

Recycling, clean energy, environmental laws, and education can reduce pollution.
