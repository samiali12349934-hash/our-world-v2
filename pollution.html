What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into the environment. It damages air, water, and land.

Types of Pollution

  • Air Pollution – Smoke and harmful gases from factories and vehicles.
  • Water Pollution – Waste and chemicals dumped into rivers and oceans.
  • Soil Pollution – Harmful waste in land and farming areas.
  • Noise Pollution – Excessive unwanted sound.
  • Light Pollution – Artificial light affecting ecosystems.

Health Effects

Pollution can cause lung disease, heart problems, cancer, and waterborne illnesses.

Environmental Impact

It destroys habitats, harms animals, and reduces biodiversity.

Solutions

  • Use renewable energy
  • Reduce plastic use
  • Recycle waste
  • Use public transportation
