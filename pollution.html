Pollution

Pollution happens when harmful substances enter the environment. Air pollution comes from cars and factories, affecting breathing. Water pollution damages rivers, lakes, and oceans. Plastic waste harms fish and wildlife. Industrial chemicals contaminate soil and food chains. Noise and light pollution disrupt natural rhythms. Emission controls can reduce pollution significantly. Recycling reduces waste and conserves resources. Renewable energy sources help cut pollution. Public awareness encourages cleaner habits. Every small action helps protect our planet.
