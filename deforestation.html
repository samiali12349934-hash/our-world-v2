Deforestation

What is Deforestation?

Deforestation is the removal of forests for agriculture, logging, and development.

Causes

Agriculture expansion, logging, mining, and infrastructure projects are major causes.

Environmental Impact

Loss of forests increases carbon dioxide and destroys habitats.

Global Hotspots

Amazon Rainforest, Congo Basin, and Southeast Asia face severe forest loss.

Solutions

Reforestation and sustainable land management are essential.
