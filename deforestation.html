🌳 Deforestation

What is Deforestation?

Deforestation is the clearing of forests for agriculture, urban development, or logging. It disrupts ecosystems and contributes to climate change.

Causes of Deforestation

  • Logging for timber and furniture
  • Agricultural expansion
  • Urbanization and infrastructure
  • Mining activities
  • Natural disasters and forest fires

Effects of Deforestation

Loss of biodiversity, soil erosion, disruption of water cycles, contribution to global warming, and displacement of indigenous communities.

Ways to Prevent Deforestation

Plant trees, promote sustainable logging, protect forest reserves, support eco-friendly products, and raise awareness about forest conservation.
