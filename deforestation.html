Deforestation

Deforestation is when large areas of trees are cut down. Trees are removed for farming and building. This destroys animal habitats and biodiversity. Carbon dioxide increases without trees absorbing it. Soil erosion becomes worse without roots holding it. Rainforests are especially rich in life and threatened. Replanting trees helps restore forests and ecosystems. Public policies can protect natural forests. Community awareness reduces deforestation. Protecting forests protects the future of the planet.
