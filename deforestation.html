Deforestation

What is Deforestation?

Deforestation is removal of forests for agriculture, logging, and development.

Causes

Agriculture expansion, logging, mining, infrastructure projects.

Environmental Impact

Loss of forests increases CO2 and destroys habitats.

Global Hotspots

Amazon Rainforest, Congo Basin, Southeast Asia.

Solutions

Reforestation & sustainable land management.
