What is Deforestation?

Deforestation is the large-scale removal of forests for agriculture, industry, and urban expansion.

Main Causes

  • Logging
  • Agriculture expansion
  • Cattle farming
  • Urban growth

Effects

  • Climate change acceleration
  • Loss of biodiversity
  • Soil erosion
  • Disruption of water cycle

Global Examples

The Amazon Rainforest and Congo Basin face severe deforestation threats.

Solutions

Reforestation, sustainable farming, and strict environmental laws help protect forests.
