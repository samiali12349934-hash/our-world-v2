What is Deforestation?

Removal of forests for farming, logging, or urban development.

Causes

  • Logging
  • Farming expansion
  • Cattle grazing
  • Urban growth

Effects

  • Climate change acceleration
  • Biodiversity loss
  • Soil erosion
  • Water cycle disruption

Solutions

Reforestation, sustainable farming, and environmental laws protect forests.
