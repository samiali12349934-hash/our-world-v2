Battle of Adwa (1896)

Background

Italy attempted to colonize Ethiopia in the late 19th century.

Leadership

Emperor Menelik II united Ethiopian forces against Italy.

The Battle

On March 1, 1896, Ethiopian forces defeated the Italian army at Adwa.

Importance

The victory preserved Ethiopia's independence and inspired African resistance movements.
