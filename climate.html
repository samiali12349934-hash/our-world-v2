Climate & Climate Change

Climate vs Weather

Weather describes short-term atmospheric conditions like rain or sunshine. Climate refers to long-term weather patterns over many years.

Types of Climate

Major climate zones include Tropical, Dry, Temperate, Continental, and Polar. Each zone has unique temperature and rainfall patterns.

Causes of Climate Change

Burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial activities increase greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide. These gases trap heat in the atmosphere.

Global Effects

Climate change causes melting glaciers, rising sea levels, extreme storms, droughts, and disruption of ecosystems.

Solutions

Renewable energy, reforestation, reducing carbon emissions, and sustainable living are key solutions.
