Climate Change

Climate change refers to long-term changes in weather patterns and temperatures. It is mainly caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels. Greenhouse gases trap heat in the atmosphere and cause global warming. Sea levels are rising because polar ice is melting. Extreme weather events are becoming more frequent. Climate resilience and adaptation are vital for communities. Renewable energy can reduce carbon emissions. Protecting forests helps absorb carbon dioxide. Public education increases climate awareness. Actions taken today shape tomorrow’s future.
