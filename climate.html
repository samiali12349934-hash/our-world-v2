What is Climate?

Climate refers to long-term weather patterns over decades. It includes temperature, rainfall, humidity, and wind.

Climate Zones

  • Tropical – Hot and humid year-round.
  • Dry – Very little rainfall.
  • Temperate – Moderate temperatures.
  • Continental – Cold winters, warm summers.
  • Polar – Extremely cold all year.

Causes of Climate Change

  • Burning fossil fuels
  • Deforestation
  • Industrial emissions
  • Transportation pollution
  • Agriculture methane

Effects

  • Rising temperatures
  • Melting glaciers
  • Sea level rise
  • Extreme weather
  • Loss of biodiversity

Solutions

Switch to renewable energy, plant trees, reduce waste, and promote sustainability to combat climate change.
