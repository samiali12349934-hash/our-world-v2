🌡 Climate of the World

What is Climate?

Climate is the long‑term pattern of weather in a particular area. It includes temperature, rainfall, humidity, and wind over years. Understanding climate helps with agriculture, water resources, and ecosystems.

Global Warming & Climate Change

Human activities like burning fossil fuels and deforestation increase greenhouse gases, causing global warming. This leads to melting ice caps, rising sea levels, and extreme weather events.

How to Protect the Climate

Plant trees, conserve water, reduce energy use, support renewable energy, and educate communities. Global action is needed to protect future generations.
