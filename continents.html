🌍 The Seven Continents of the World

🌍 Africa

Countries: 54

Population: Over 1.4 billion

Africa is the second-largest continent. It is known as the birthplace of humanity. It contains the Sahara Desert, the Nile River, and rich cultural diversity. Many ancient civilizations started here including Egypt and Ethiopia.

🌎 North America

Major Countries: USA, Canada, Mexico

North America has powerful economies and diverse climates. It ranges from Arctic cold in Canada to tropical regions in Mexico.

🌎 South America

Major Countries: Brazil, Argentina, Colombia

Home to the Amazon Rainforest and Andes Mountains. Most countries speak Spanish or Portuguese.

🌍 Europe

Countries: About 44

Europe played a major role in world history including colonization and both World Wars. It has strong economies and rich cultural history.

🌏 Asia

Largest continent by size and population.

Includes China, India, Japan, and Middle Eastern countries. It has diverse cultures, religions, and economies.

🌏 Australia

Australia is both a country and a continent. It has unique wildlife like kangaroos and koalas.

❄ Antarctica

The coldest continent. No permanent population. Used mainly for scientific research.
